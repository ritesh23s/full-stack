Vertical Alignment..?

⇛ Whith the help of following classes we can align the elements in Verticaly.

  1. .align-item-start ⇒ Align in start (top)
  2. .align-item-center ⇒ Align in center
  3. .align-item-end ⇒ Align in end (buttom)
